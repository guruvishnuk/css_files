Pav Bhaji

A famous Mumbai street food, Pav Bhaji is a spicy vegetable mash served with buttered pav (bread rolls). Perfect for a quick and satisfying meal!

ü•ï Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, potato)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 1 green bell pepper (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Pav (bread rolls)

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions:

  1. Boil the mixed vegetables until soft and mash them together.
  2. Heat butter in a pan, add cumin seeds, ginger-garlic paste, and onions. Cook until onions are golden.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, bell pepper, and pav bhaji masala. Cook for a few minutes.
  4. Mix in the mashed vegetables, salt, and cook for 5 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Toast pav in butter and serve the bhaji hot with the toasted pav and a sprinkle of coriander leaves.
