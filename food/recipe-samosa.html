Samosa

Golden crispy pastry filled with spicy mashed potatoes and peas, a beloved Indian snack!

ü•ü Dough Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for kneading)

ü•î Filling Ingredients:

  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Oil for frying

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, salt, and oil. Add water gradually and knead into a firm dough. Cover and let rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, green chilies, mashed potatoes, peas, and spices. Mix and cool the filling.
  3. Divide dough into balls. Roll into ovals, cut in half, form cones, fill with stuffing, and seal edges with water.
  4. Deep fry on medium heat until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney or tamarind sauce!
