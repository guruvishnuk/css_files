Butter Naan

Soft and fluffy naan brushed with butter, perfect with Indian curries and tandoori dishes!

üçû Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp butter (for brushing)

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add yogurt, milk, sugar, and oil. Knead into a soft dough and let it rest for 2 hours.
  3. Divide dough into balls, roll into oval shapes.
  4. Cook on hot tawa or pan till golden brown. Flip and cook other side.
  5. Brush with butter and serve hot with curry.
