Palak Paneer

A healthy and delicious Indian curry made with fresh spinach and cottage cheese (paneer).

ü•¨ Ingredients:

  • 250g fresh spinach leaves
  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste

  1. Blanch spinach leaves in boiling water for 2 minutes, then transfer to ice water. Puree the spinach and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan and saut√© cumin seeds, chopped onions, and green chili until golden brown.
  3. Add pureed tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the spinach puree and cook for another 5-7 minutes until the mixture thickens.
  5. Gently add the paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes. Add fresh cream and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with naan or rice.
