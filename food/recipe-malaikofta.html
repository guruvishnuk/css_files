Malai Kofta

A rich and creamy curry made with deep-fried koftas (dumplings) in a flavorful gravy!

ü•î Ingredients:

  • 1 cup paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1/2 cup boiled potatoes
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or oil for frying
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions:

  1. For the koftas: Mash paneer and boiled potatoes together. Add cornflour, garam masala, cumin powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Shape into small balls and deep fry until golden brown. Set aside.
  2. For the gravy: Heat oil in a pan, saut√© onions and ginger-garlic paste until golden. Add pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  3. Add heavy cream and cook for 5 minutes. Add water if needed to adjust consistency.
  4. Carefully add the fried koftas to the gravy and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with naan or rice.
