Aloo Paratha

Delicious stuffed flatbread filled with spicy mashed potatoes, perfect for breakfast or lunch!

ü•î Ingredients:

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 3 large boiled potatoes
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Ghee or oil for roasting

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions:

  1. Knead the wheat flour with water to make soft dough. Set aside for 20 minutes.
  2. Mash boiled potatoes and mix in chili, cumin, garam masala, chili powder, salt, and coriander.
  3. Divide dough into balls, roll each slightly, place filling in the center, seal, and roll again gently.
  4. Heat a pan and roast each paratha with ghee until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with curd, butter, or pickle.
