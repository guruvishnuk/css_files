Masala Dosa

A crispy, golden-brown crepe filled with a spiced mashed potato filling, served with chutney and sambar.

ü•î Ingredients (for the dosa batter):

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup split urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Water as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for roasting

üç† Ingredients (for the potato filling):

  • 4 medium boiled potatoes
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala (optional)

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Instructions:

  1. For the dosa batter: Wash rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds. Soak them in water for 6-8 hours. Grind them into a smooth batter with some water. Let it ferment for 12-24 hours.
  2. For the filling: Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Add chopped onions, chilies, and ginger. Fry until onions are translucent. Add turmeric powder, mashed potatoes, salt, and garam masala. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes. Add chopped coriander leaves and set aside.
  3. To make the dosa: Heat a tawa or griddle and grease with oil. Pour a ladle of batter and spread it thin. Cook on both sides until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Place the potato filling in the center, fold the dosa, and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
