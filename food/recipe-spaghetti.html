Spaghetti Bolognese

A rich and hearty Italian pasta dish made with ground beef, tomatoes, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 150g Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 400g Tomato Sauce
  • Olive Oil, Salt, Pepper, Basil

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until golden.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and basil. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Mix sauce with spaghetti and serve hot with grated cheese.
